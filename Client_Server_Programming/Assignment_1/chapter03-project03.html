Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Devin Hopkins

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean
Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Rivière
family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions 100 x 70 cm
